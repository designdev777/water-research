The Investment Thesis

The Problem: A $200B Blind Spot

Governments and corporations are committing billions to carbon sequestration and regenerative agriculture, but they lack the cheap, verifiable data to:

  • Price Carbon Accurately: Current soil carbon measurement can cost >$10/hectare, making smallholder projects unviable.
  • Verify Regenerative Claims: "Greenwashing" is rampant due to a lack of transparent, real-time soil health data.
  • Deploy Climate Finance Efficiently: Funds are allocated based on models, not ground-truth data, leading to wasted capital.
The Solution: Proprietary Soil Intelligence

We leverage a breakthrough scientific insight: Soil CO₂ respiration dynamics are a primary indicator of overall soil health and carbon flux. Our platform combines:

  • Hardware: Ruggedized, low-power sensor nodes measuring CO₂, O₂, moisture, and temperature at multiple depths.
  • Analytics: Proprietary algorithms that translate gas fluxes into actionable metrics (Carbon Sequestration Score, Microbial Activity Index, Drought Resilience Metric).
  • Satellite Integration: Fuses ground data with free satellite (NDVI) for scalable verification.

Our Unique Advantage: We measure the process (respiration), not just the state (carbon stock), allowing for predictive insights and earlier intervention.
