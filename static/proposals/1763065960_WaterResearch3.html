The Investment Thesis: Solving a $1B+ Problem

AquaPulse Analytics is a tech-enabled service company targeting the chronic failure of rural water infrastructure in Sub-Saharan Africa. We turn a cost center into a profitable, data-driven asset.

The Core Problem: 30-40% of rural handpumps in Africa are non-functional at any given time, representing a multi-billion dollar wasted investment and denying millions of clean water.

The AquaPulse Solution: A low-cost, open-source hardware and software platform that provides real-time, remote monitoring of water usage and pump performance. This enables:

  • Predictive Maintenance: Fixing pumps before they break.
  • Data-Driven Operations: Optimizing maintenance routes and resource allocation.
  • Guaranteed Uptime: Offering "Water as a Service" to governments and NGOs.

ROI & Exit Strategy: The High-Yield Pathways

The goal is not to build a billion-dollar company with the seed round. It is to use £10,000 to create an asset so compelling that it demands acquisition or licensing by a major player within 18-24 months.

Pathway 1: Government License

Target: National Government (e.g., Ghana's CWSA).

Deal: One-time license fee of £250k-£500k for the IP, playbook, and training to roll out nationally.

Seed ROI Multiplier: 25x - 50x

Pathway 2: Private Sector Acquisition

Target: Major WASH NGO (WaterAid, World Vision) or Water Tech Firm.

Deal: Acquisition for £500k-£1.5M to integrate the proven system into their global operations.

Seed ROI Multiplier: 50x - 150x

Pathway 3: Social Impact Franchise

Target: Social Enterprise Fund.

Deal: Sell regional franchise licenses across Africa, creating a recurring revenue stream valued at £2M+.

Seed ROI Multiplier: 200x+
