Investment Thesis

Core Strategy: Use £10,000 to generate irrefutable, field-validated proof that our GIS-MCDA model dramatically reduces the cost and risk of drilling for high-value customers.

The Opportunity

In water-stressed regions like the Lake Chad Basin, high-fidelity, actionable data on groundwater is a valuable and currently undersupplied commodity. We're not selling water; we're selling the knowledge of where to find it efficiently.

Target Markets

  • AgTech & Commercial Farming: De-risking land acquisition and drilling campaigns
  • Mining Companies: Securing water supply for remote operations
  • Government & NGOs: Improving success rates of public water projects
  • Insurance & Finance: Data for risk assessment in water-dependent ventures

Why This Works

The methodology combines NASA satellite data (GRACE, SRTM), geological surveys, and field validation to create predictive groundwater potential maps with >80% accuracy.
